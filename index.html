We care about you being GDPR compliant!

Why you should care, too…

  • Cookies are undoubtable part of GDPR

    Cookie identifiers are governed by GDPR according to GDPR Rec.30 as they can be used to identify users or create profiles.

  • No external cookies without user consent

    Without proven consent, you are not allowed to load external code, e.g. retargeting and tracking provider.

  • Option to dissent must be clear and prominent

    You must NOT load any external tags without user consent. To dissent must be as easy as to consent according to GDPR Art.18 (e.g. one click).

  • Do not track users below age of 16

    According to GDPR Art.8 users that are to be tracked must be 16 years or older. If they are not (considered “child”) you need to receive consent of a legal guardian or you must not load any external tags on your site.
